ACME Corporation Employee Handbook
===================================

Last Updated: January 2025

Chapter 1: Time Off Policies
----------------------------

Annual Leave (PTO)
- All full-time employees receive 15 days of paid time off per year
- Employees with 5+ years of service receive 20 days per year
- Employees with 10+ years of service receive 25 days per year
- PTO requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance
- Unused PTO can be carried over up to 5 days maximum

Sick Leave
- Employees receive 10 sick days per year
- Sick days do not carry over to the next year
- A doctor's note is required for absences of 3 or more consecutive days

Holidays
- The company observes 10 paid holidays per year
- Holiday schedule is published in December for the following year
- If a holiday falls on a weekend, it will be observed on the nearest weekday

Chapter 2: Remote Work Policy
-----------------------------

Eligibility
- All employees who have completed their probationary period (90 days) are eligible
- Some positions may require on-site presence based on job requirements
- Remote work requests must be approved by your direct manager

Equipment
- The company provides a laptop for all remote workers
- Employees are responsible for their home internet connection
- A monthly stipend of $50 is provided for home office expenses

Work Hours
- Core hours are 10 AM to 3 PM in your local timezone
- You must be available for meetings during core hours
- Total work hours should equal your standard workweek (40 hours for full-time)

Chapter 3: Expense Reimbursement
--------------------------------

Travel Expenses
- All business travel must be pre-approved by your manager
- Economy class is standard for flights under 6 hours
- Business class requires VP approval for flights over 6 hours
- Hotel expenses are reimbursed up to $200 per night in major cities
- Meal expenses are reimbursed up to $75 per day

Professional Development
- Each employee has a $2,000 annual budget for professional development
- This can be used for courses, conferences, books, and certifications
- Requests should be submitted through the HR portal

Office Supplies
- Standard office supplies are provided by the company
- Specialized equipment requests require manager approval
- Ergonomic equipment (standing desks, chairs) is available upon request with HR approval

Chapter 4: Benefits
-------------------

Health Insurance
- Medical, dental, and vision coverage is provided
- The company pays 80% of premiums for employee coverage
- The company pays 60% of premiums for family coverage
- Coverage begins on the first day of the month following your start date

401(k) Retirement Plan
- Employees can contribute up to IRS maximum limits
- The company matches 100% of contributions up to 4% of salary
- Vesting is immediate for your contributions
- Company match vests over 3 years (33% per year)

Life Insurance
- Basic life insurance of 1x annual salary is provided at no cost
- Supplemental life insurance is available for purchase

Chapter 5: Code of Conduct
--------------------------

Professionalism
- Treat all colleagues with respect and dignity
- Maintain confidentiality of company and client information
- Avoid conflicts of interest
- Report any ethics concerns to HR or use the anonymous hotline

Communication
- Respond to emails within 24 business hours
- Use appropriate channels for different types of communication
- Be mindful of time zones when scheduling meetings

Dress Code
- Business casual is the standard dress code
- Client-facing meetings may require business professional attire
- Casual Fridays allow jeans and casual shirts
